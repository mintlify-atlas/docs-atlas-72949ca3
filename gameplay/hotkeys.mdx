fheroes2 provides keyboard shortcuts for every major action in the game — from navigating the adventure map to managing armies in battle. All hotkeys are fully configurable: you can view the current assignments and remap any key from within the game itself. The tables below list the default assignments organized by context.

<Tip>
  To view or remap hotkeys in-game, open **System Options** (default: `O` on the adventure map) and look for the **Hot Keys** button. Every binding shown in the tables below can be changed from that screen.
</Tip>

## Android touch controls

On Android, the touchscreen replaces the mouse and keyboard. Several gestures simulate actions that would normally require a right-click or keyboard shortcut.

**Right-click (info popup)**

Touch and hold the item you want to inspect, then touch anywhere else on the screen with a second finger. While the second finger is held down you can remove the first finger and the info window stays open.

**Map panning**

Border scrolling is disabled by default on Android. To pan the adventure map, press anywhere on the map area and slide your finger to move the view.

**Combat actions**

Battle actions use a two-tap confirmation system:

1. Tap once to place the cursor on a target cell. The game shows information about the action (movement path, attack angle, affected cells, and potential damage).
2. Tap a second time to confirm and execute the action — but only if the action matches what was displayed in step 1.

**Selecting attack angle**

To control the angle of a melee attack on a touchscreen, swipe from the cell you want to attack from to the cell occupied by the enemy unit, then lift your finger. Verify the displayed attack and confirm by tapping the target cell again.

<Note>
  Fullscreen mode is on by default on Android. To show the system info bar with the clock, disable fullscreen mode in **Main Menu → Settings → Graphics**.
</Note>
